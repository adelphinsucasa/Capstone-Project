The best web course believing in the value of openness and sharing, creating a positive change with people from over 80 countries is taking place in January, in Microverse.

2023.01.09(MON) ~ 13(FRI)

Main Program

  • Lecture

    Listen to the speakers from various countries about the messages of sharing and opening.
